TALKING PLANTS

by Olivia Mevorach

I really loved making this video, as soon as it came to me I hurriedly painted the "sets" and cut up the wonky speech bubbles.































































Dialogue:

Silver Pothos: The dad's a drunk who's hardly around but they love him still.

Philodendren: Are all of her paintings about her traumas/ insecurities? They all had the same face.

Jade Plant:Have you ever wondered? If there are things you don't remember?

Philodendren:Doesn't sound very nutritious

Silver Pothos: One time I saw the moon become a sky God.

Philodendren: Cool...Cool guy.

Jade Plant: In some alternate dimension...

Philodendren:In this one too.

Silver Pothos:You always know just what to say.

Jade Plant: Yeah. Watch his beer.

Philodendren:He melted it, froze it, and resold it.

Jade Plant: Fertilizer?

Silver Pothos:Cream in space.

Silver Pothos:You hold on to what maintains your sanity and either forget or repress what would destroy it.

Philodendren:What's ultimately important is that we both believe in a world of green and brown.

Jade Plant:How do you properly stretch your hip-flexors?

Silver Pothos:House Hunters just brings out the like, need for normalcy around me.

Silver Pothos:I dont think I need a man...but just in case. Where is he?!

Philodendren:He's a foe of the East Coast.

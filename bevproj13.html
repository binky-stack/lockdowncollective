Arbus Twins in Wormwood Scrubs

by Beverly Frydman

identical
side by side
not holding hands
hands almost touching

twin on the right smiles
the one on the left does not
no hairbands or tights
not black and white

barefoot in red dresses
they stand on a log
long hair in pigtails
they stare straight ahead

mother
holds up a phone
nobody moves
until she says click

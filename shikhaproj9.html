I hate the hour before I fly

by Shikha Subramaniam

Scum of the earth ➡ all of humanity
packed into one air train
towards our collective doom - JFK

Train lines blend
into a long, endless python winding
slowly & stealthily through security check
and dogs galloping between the snakes, sniffing, snuffling.

Everybody seems to have done this a million times,
but nobody seems to remember how.
I sigh at the gestalt of this – the sum
of parts really is other than the whole:

I really do love being places,
but it always makes me sick
to get there.

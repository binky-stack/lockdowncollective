Untitled

by Dan Frydman

You have to take big, purposeful steps to get yourself out of this negative state. When you decide to sit down and organize your life, make you sure you have a recording of yourself taking big, purposeful steps. If you find yourself blaming your mood on the weather, or groceries, then write them a letter. While waiting for a reply, ask google what happens after we die. Even if you can afford one, go out at night and steal a turtle. Consider this a warning.

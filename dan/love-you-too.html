Love you too

by Dan Frydman

Grandma says steam is the cure, the lime juice is just something I’m trying
Thank you for doing the dishes, now please introduce that dog to duck tape
Let’s kiss intelligently, with our lips tucked-in
I appreciate the sentiment, but could you not scratch my feet with floss picks
I did wear my mask outside, but I also may have accidentally licked a mailbox
Let me dust crumbs off so I can…continue to love you
It was such an overthetop performance; she might as well have moonwalked
Look at what my followers think about the holes in your underwear
Excuse me, that was the recently changed shirt, thank you very much
Of course you can jumprope, after you find somewhere else to live

What I Gather from the Black & White Photo of Grandma & Grandpa

by Beverly Frydman

No red in her hair
eyes do not shift
from green to grey
like angry oceans

His hair is black
true to youth
pepper no salt
that came much later

She doesn’t wear
coke bottle glasses
a look he prefers
she likes him blurry

How young are they
she has no wrinkles
he is not stupid
don’t need a ketubah

to say they got married
that she was from old town
not new town ghetto
that always defined him

They smile a little
stare straight ahead
never look back
nothing is there

Passover Seder at Grandma Clara’s

by Beverly Frydman

I hear whispers the gefilte
fish swam in the bathtub
before I got here

How could that be
It would sink even though
it looks like clouds

with cooked carrot hats
that I think is feh
Grandma says ess ess

At home gefilte fish
comes from a jar
of murky water

it is not sweet
like grandmas which
she says is the best

I go to pee
nothing fishy here
just bleach, perfume, hair oil

a whiff of Grandma’s shit
which also smells sweet
It must be the best

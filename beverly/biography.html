Biography of Me

by Beverly Frydman

Bites her nails when bored, frightened or buzzing over Basquiat
Escapes into flights of fancy and whimsy, she's ridiculous
Virtual atheist, posts her mail-in-ballot chanting please god please god please god
Even though last time she kissed her ballot and the whole world lost
Rabbit eared sneakers walk her to the park, she finds poetry in overflowing bins
Loves sneaky G&Ts and Lindt extra creamy but not together
Yawns a lot, doesn't sleep, dreams daily

Mood Board

by Beverly Frydman

Don’t ask me if I’m in a mood
I’m prickly
I was woken up at 3:00 am
by a fart

I need a strategy
my head is full of weeds
I can’t stop eating ice cream
I can’t stop thinking about death

I want to swear all the time
I need to pull myself together
I had two gin and tonics and a glass of wine
I am also bored

I guess it will pass
I take comfort in my one grey eyebrow hair
I have no message
I am in pieces

I smell like soy sauce
I love tights
I have a scarf and pearls on
I have to stop watching The Crown
I don’t know which lightbulbs to buy
I am in the dark
I sent my mail in ballot back
I am an optimist

Don’t ask me if I’m in a mood
isn’t everyone
I grew up in a valley
I’m in one again

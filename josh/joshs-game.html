"YOUR TURN"

by Josh Frydman & Olivia Mevorach

Olivia decides to indulge Josh in a friendly game of "Your turn"
Josh is a ferocious player, taking no mercy on his pupil (Olivia), speaking 100mpm in order to swirl her brain into a thick mush. Unfortunately for Mr. Josh, the high of dominating in his favorite game soon falls apart in fragments, as Olivia plays her hand, his world crumbles. Josh shows us his true colors by abandoning his party hat.

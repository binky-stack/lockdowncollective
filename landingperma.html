  • Our Statement

    Our digital platform will address and frame analytically discussions in the field of gender/feminist studies. We start with examining the theoretical approaches in gender/feminist studies by using “intersectionality theory,” as a guiding framework. Inclusion of ideas and experiences allow us to look beyond the class “reading” and apply them to everyday life. By combining our class readings, exploring our own work and life experiences at home and in our communities at large, we will further our collective understanding and engagement in promoting equity, equality, and empowerment for all.

Poke

by Kate Frydman

Don’t poke the feeling
For what’s inside
Demons don’t leave when seen
They fake a cigarette break

Talk to yourself behind your back
Swivel around and around
You’ll know when to stop

Forget only when your memory is full of music

Cherish the lies you tell yourself,
Give them names and faces and write their happy endings
The hurt is in your friend
They met because they both loved you.

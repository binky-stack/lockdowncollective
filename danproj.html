[nightmoves]

by Dan Frydman

the clap of dress shoes on pavement rudely interrupts
a scavenging family of rats agonizingly investigating
styrofoam-filled recyclables causing them to flee
across the street one at at time as if crossing
no mans land when a bump and a screech
and a revving receding is followed by
the jingle of a collar approaching
as a creaking pub sign swings
over the returned traveler
whistling a smiths song
while unwrapping
his candy bar.

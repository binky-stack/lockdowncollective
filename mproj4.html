The Rarest Commodity

by Michael Frydman

Palladium, once platinum’s poor cousin,
and thus often ignored
Was found perfect for EV
So it’s value soared

Rare Earths are another
Lumped together in shame
Then they’re needed in IPhones
And they lived up to their name

Last year, it was Vanadium
Once solely for steel-making
Then battery flow
Sent the market price shaking

But then there is water
Once thought so abundant
Now so rare that the search for the others,
Seems sadly redundant

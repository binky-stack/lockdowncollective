Flyfishing in Bed

by Dan Frydman

Tugged sharply by the urge to pull — as joy


subsides, like one lap too many — like


shrinking windows, or stomach acid,


or playing seesaw on a second hand coffee table.


I nabbed a fly in the bathroom mirror. Serenaded


dishes with disinfectant. Exercised kindness


with inbred cables. Was interrupted, with disturbing


continuity, by a fucking cottage cheese commercial.

Morning People

by Kate Frydman

EXT. LOS ANGELES COMPLEX. DAY

JACK (27)
a Tik Tok star, hops down the steps of his two story complex, breathing in the afternoon air.

JACK (sotto)
You’re unique and you’re special. They need you. They all need you.

Jack jumps to the bottom and does a burpee. He pops back up, out of breath. Jack walks the courtyard confidently, he passes by MRS PEREZ smoking parliaments in her rocking chair, aggressively rolling over her rose garden .

JACK (CONT'D)
Morning Mrs Perez!

MRS PEREZ
That’s what you think, you baboon!

JACK
Well alright then!

MRS PEREZ
Freakin moron.

She rips one of the roses right out the ground, and tosses it at him.
Jack continues on his way. He sees a commotion at the end of the court yard. Instinctively, he goes for his phone, starts recording.

ON JACK’S PHONE: Jack approaches the small crowd of neighbors. They’re fit, good looking twenty somethings, all filming personal testimonials on their own phones. He continues to talk to his viewers.

SHERRY (20s)
has a concern look on her perfectly contoured face, talks into her phone.

SHERRY
I came down here and was like what’s going on? And then I saw what was going on. And I was still like, ‘what’?

JACK
Never a dull moment here.

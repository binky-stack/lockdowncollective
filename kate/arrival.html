Arrival Scene

by Kate Frydman

INT. SUITE 5B.
.... Stevvanie ushers Connie to the sofa, pours her a glass of lemon water. Charlie and Aubrey look on cautiously.

CONNIE
It's so nice to finally meet you all, I've heard so much about you.

They all nod silently, giving her nothing.

CONNIE (CONT)
What a lovely welcome party. That's so like Clayton to organize this... I think?-- No, what am I saying? It's definitely like him.

Beat.

CONNIE (CONT)
Is he stuck at the office or--

AUBREY
The office...? No. No, Clayton's not here.

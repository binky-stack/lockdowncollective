Gossip Queen

by Josh Frydman

INT. BEDROOM - NIGHT
Roy is sleeping in his bed. Suddenly, a bright white light
flashes onto him and startles him awake.
Roy sits up in his bed to look upon a great white flashing
light, a ball of beautiful energy.

ROY
Come on man, I have work in the morning.

GOD
You know who’s a bitch?

It’s god.
Roy puts his head down on his pillow in a vain attempt to go back to sleep. GOD (CONT’D)
Seriously though, you know who’s
been acting like a total bitch
these days?

ROY
I’m not engaging.

GOD
Trust me, you’re gonna wanna hear
this, it is juicy.

ROY
I’ve never met any of the people
you tell me about!

GOD
Fine I’ll just tell you then.
It’s Jose de Paula.

ROY
That’s a stranger!

GOD
Well let me tell you then, you are
lucky.
You wouldn’t want anything to do with this old bitch I promise you.
He’s 68, he’s an accountant,
and he complains to me every day that people find him boring.
It’s like, new flash,
you’re a boring old accountant.
It’s like Jesus, find a new slant, you know!

ROY
No! I don’t know what you’re
talking about so can you please let
me sleep for once!

GOD
Yeah a lot of people have just been on my ass recently you know.
The other day, Ryan Oscar Mattley was just begging me to make him
straight.
Can you imagine? I’m just sick of people thinking I have like magical powers or something.

ROY
You’re God!

GOD
I gotta say though, I’m so grateful have someone to talk to.
I’d go crazy otherwise, I really would.

ROY
What a minute-

GOD
Everyone needs a space to speak
their truth.

Roy sits up.

ROY
- You don’t do this to anyone else?

GOD
There’s been a real trend recently of people about the last season of
The Gilmore Girls for some reason.

ROY
Hey, answer my question!

GOD
Oh, okay, rude then.

ROY
Do you do this to anyone else?

GOD
What?

ROY
Do you, like, invade other people’s homes and keep them awake with
gossip?

GOD
Why would I do that with other
people, I have you.

ROY
But why me?!

GOD
You’re just such a great listener.

ROY
No I’m not!

GOD
And you’re so humble too,
I love that about you.

“ – Grass and Grape Soda”

by Shikha Subramaniam

I
barely get this sentence out;
you:
already whisked away
we skip about a hundred paces
by the way your lips curve upward,
lifting a remote desire
from the depths
of that one night in spring
when you and your lover felt
nothing but fireflies
breathing on your skin

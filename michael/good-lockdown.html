A Good Lockdown

by Michael Frydman

Fair cop: I’ve been one of the lucky (silent) minority that has had a good lockdown. Like some consultants, creative workers, Joe Biden and resume editors (diplomas as well), I not only don’t miss my job as defined by an office environment, truth is I’m kind of thriving outside of it. I mostly trade a variety of financial instruments on-line in an effort to make money (call me a ‘day trader’ at your peril) and this C19-led volatility has been relatively kind to me. Meantime, not that I care much about social norms, it’s still nice to note sometimes that trading financial markets in your pajamas from a laptop isn’t viewed as quite as sad as it used to be. Take that - at least for the next 10 weeks - all you fancy pants lawyers in fancy pants.

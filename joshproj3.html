KILLER PLANTS

by Josh Frydman and Olivia Mevorach

Story and Art Direction by Olivia, Illustrated by Josh



in the tiny town of sea cliff
it was always sunny
or so it would seem...

every 5 years killer plantts rise, taking the place of existing nature...
and devouring the heads of random townspeople
the townspeople tried to take preventative measures
they chopped down the trees
trampled the flowerbeds
until all the green was gone

and yet, nature grew back
more bountiful than before
so the people waited and waited for a miracle
tick...tick...

god never came
from the on, every five years, on the first of june, the townspeople gathered their weapons and prepared...
...to fight!
one day...

a little boy turned to his father: "why must today be unlinke any other day, papa? can't we just stay inside?"/
papa thought long and hard...
and decided they'd stay in: "i know! to be safe, we shall stay indoors"

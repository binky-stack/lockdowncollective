The Minute I Get Home

by Beverly Frydman

Burst through the door
after school, free the tears
they’ve been waiting all day
Safe

Run, trip up stairs
unzip, sit on toilet
release a sigh and steaming pee
they’ve been patient long enough
Relief

Charge to the kitchen
grab remaining bbq chips
spent the day behaving
Starving

Rush to the mailbox and back
three minutes round trip from home
dog greets me like a prodigal
Lockdown

The Lockdown Collective's website features the work of; Beverly Frydman, Michael Frydman, Kate Frydman, Josh Frydman, Dan Frydman, Shikha Subramaniam, and Olivia Mevorach. The group gathers for Zoom meetings every Saturday to present the work they've done during the week. This website was created for the purpose of organizing and showcasing the art made by the collective during this specific time.

About the Group:

The Lockdown Collective was formed by the poet Beverly Frydman.
The group gathers every Saturday, 2:00pm EST, 11:00am PST, and 7:00pm London Time :~).
During our Zoom-gatherings, each member presents a new creation; a fun, or somber, or educational work that they've completed during the week.
TLC's website features work from;
Beverly Frydman, Michael Frydman, Kate Frydman, Josh Frydman, Dan Frydman, Shikha Subramaniam, and Olivia Mevorach.
This website was created for the purpose of organizing and showcasing the art made by the collective during this specific time.

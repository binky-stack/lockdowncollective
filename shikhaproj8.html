by Shikha Subramaniam

INT - KITCHEN, 12:43 AM
DOT AND ODIE are sitting ACROSS FROM EACH OTHER at the KITCHEN TABLE.

DOT
So, what is the best way to kill someone, do you think?

ODIE
You mean the best way to kill him?

DOT
An eye for an eye, right, why fucking not?

ODIE
What exactly is the "eye" here?

DOT
The first eye or the second eye?

ODIE
Well the first eye is murder.

DOT
Yeah, I could get away with it, after some research maybe.

ODIE
You're too far away.

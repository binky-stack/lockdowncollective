INT. AIRPLANE - DAY

Connie is in the very back row of a massive Boeing 747. The air pressure roars as the cabin rattles nauseatingly, drinks spill into the aisle, babies scream freely in the arms of delirious parents.
Connie is in intense, sweaty conversation with GRAHAM CHARLTON (50s) a ruddy faced Englishman, gay, shit-faced and loving Connie's energy.

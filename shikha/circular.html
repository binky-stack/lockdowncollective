So it seems...Naturally (ACT I)

by Shikha Subramaniam

INT - KITCHEN, 12:43 AM
DOT AND ODIE are sitting ACROSS FROM EACH OTHER at the KITCHEN TABLE.

DOT
So, what is the best way to kill someone, do you think?

ODIE
You mean the best way to kill him?

DOT
An eye for an eye, right, why fucking not?

ODIE
What exactly is the "eye" here?

DOT
The first eye or the second eye?

ODIE
Well the first eye is murder.

DOT
Yeah, I could get away with it, after some research maybe.

ODIE
You're too far away.

DOT
We're in a digital age, it's 2020, it's the "new normal" - shouldn't there be a way to do just about ANYTHING I want online?

ODIE
Yeah yeah, I'll throw The new normal. up if i hear that phrase one more time.

DOT
I have to find a way - My brain can't see any other way, there's no end in sight.

ODIE
Do you think his daughter will miss him? Miss knowing him?

DOT
I fucking forgot about his daughter.

ODIE
Yeah, that's a fucking doozy.

DOT
So, back to the main point here. What are we going to do with this problem?

ODIE
Right. So you have known everything for how long now?

DOT
Since January 3rd, told me SHE everything a day after her birthday, when it was just the two of us in her car.

ODIE
I wonder why . now

DOT
Because I asked her. I straight up asked her.

ODIE
You asked her what?

DOT
I asked her if - if everything was okay wi...

ODIE
- So you knew?

DOT
NO way, never!

ODIE
I guess she never saw it coming either, not always.

DOT
No, she's pretty shaken. So I gotta help her out, I have to do something.

ODIE
Help her out or yourself out?

DOT
Two birds.

ODIE
Okay, so do something, something short of murder?!

DOT
What is short of murder? Fighting? Admonishing, Screaming? Disapproving? Shaming?

ODIE
Any of those might be a better place to start, yeah.

DOT
Sure, but i can't do any of those, and even if I could I wouldn't know where to begin.

ODIE
Are you scared of him?

DOT
I don't know what I am of him, that's the problem. I'm too close to it all.

ODIE
Why not talk to him?

DOT
Yeah so that's the thing, i can't. She won't let me. She doesn't want me to tell him or anyone else anything I know.

ODIE
So if you say something then you hurt her, and if you don't do anything then you hurt yourself?

DOT
She's done so much for me, too much. I have to do this for her.

ODIE
So you're one of a handful of people who knows, but he doesn't know you know?

DOT
Sure me and about fifty strangers who were there. And no he doesn't know. He has his suspicions though, he can't look me in the eye anymore.

ODIE
Maybe he senses that you want to kill him.

DOT
I dream about him, I dream about HER, I dream about it. Every night, almost.

ODIE
I'm sorry, that's stressful. Maybe you should talk to someone, a professional?

DOT
You think I haven't tried? Every week since January my therapist and I have been trying to chip away at it.

ODIE
And? Zero progress?

DOT
No, we agreed we can't make any progress until something else happens.

ODIE
So. You can't talk to him. or tell anyone else, and he gets away with everything.

DOT
Yeah, and there's no closure or resolution, and it will just continue to haunt me when I'm asleep or awake and brushing my teeth, or driving to the studio, or shopping for groceries.

ODIE
Why can't do anything about it - She what is she doing about it?! She was there, hell she was there. right

DOT
Yeah but that's a dead end. I told you (irritated) - She's the most selfless person I know. and she hates confrontation, she can't do anything.

ODIE
She do anything. won't

DOT
No, she won't, not until she moves some money around.

ODIE
Okay, I guess you wait for her to do all that, right?

DOT
I can't, that will never happen.

ODIE
What?!

DOT
I know she won't ever do it, she will never really make moves, she doesn't want to ruffle any feathers, she's scared too.

ODIE
Yeah, I would be, I don't blame her.

ODIE
So you think it will never happen?!

DOT
No, Nothing will ever change or move and it will just torture and Her plague me for the rest of our lives.

ODIE
...and he will get away with it. I can't, I just can't deal with any of it longer.

ODIE
So you don't want to wait, but you can't do anything about it.

DOT
I can't talk to him, I can't tell anyone else, I can't make her do anything about it, and more than anything, I just can't do this any longer.

ODIE
So you have to kill him.

DOT
So it seems.

So It Seems...Naturally (ACT II)

by Shikha Subramaniam

INT - KITCHEN, 12:43 AM, ONE MONTH LATER

ODIE
So, did you think of a way to kill him?

DOT
A feww, but that seems all too simplistic now.

ODIE
I thought that's what you liked about it.

DOT
I don't want him to die anymore, I want him to suffer.

ODIE (slightly smirking)
Ah, so it's true what they say: time does heal all wounds.

DOT
Perhaps I might possibly even begin to acknowledge your facetious remark if I found that the situation was even remotely calling for hilarity.

ODIE
I apologize. So why the change of heart from murderous to sadistic?

DOT
He's moving out, or so I've heard.

ODIE
Oh that's huge. Good riddance, right?

DOT
Absolutely (hesitant)

ODIE
But...?

DOT
Nothing feels like a victory.

ODIE
I don't know, doesn't he lose everyone he loved?

DOT
With what heart?

ODIE
What does your therapist think?

DOT (gazing, distant)
I had this insidious dream where everything was normal. HE was there, SHE was too. Everything was normal, and I was me. But I was also a child at the same time
It even felt nice.

ODIE
As if nothing had happened?

DOT
No, that's the thing. It had. In the dream, I knew.

ODIE
Hm, I see 'insidious' now.

DOT
It could never play out like that in real life.

ODIE
Maybe ten years from now if you f-

DOT
Don't even say it. Not happening. SHE hasn't come this far for it to come undone.

ODIE
Okay okay. So, what does your therapist think?

DOT
She wants me to write about the good stuff.

ODIE
How's that going?

DOT
I made a list of ways to hurt him.

ODIE
Lay it on me.

DOT
Cut up all his suits, and write a death threat on his office wall with the cuttings.

ODIE
He does love all 80 of his suits.

DOT
Try about 150, he took over our closets when we left.

ODIE
Maybe kidnap his suits, and send a ransom note.

DOT (emphatically)
YES, great! Now you're getting the hang of this.

ODIE
...and we write the ransom note with what's leftover of the ties.

DOT
Make a million dollars and sign them off to everyone but him.

ODIE
Start a not him club.

DOT
Change my last name to something else.

ODIE
I wouldn't even know how to recognize you. Just drop the last name.

DOT
Write a letter to the IRDA and get him fired.

ODIE
Eat all the butterscotch ice cream in the country so he can never have any.

DOT
I can't think of much more he holds dear.

ODIE
How's your brother taking it, now that he knows?

DOT
He's an absolute champ. Handling it better than I did.

ODIE
Just give it time to sink in.

DOT
Well, I'll be here.

ODIE
Yes...with your list of ways to hurt and murder. Patent pending.

DOT
He couldn't fucking believe about the daughter. I feel bad for her, really.

ODIE
That child might grow up to be a serial killer.

DOT
I would have been so messed up if not for HER.

ODIE
Yeah, is she relieved?

DOT
No, not really. Not as much as one would think. 10 years of being trapped in a land that lays still and not saying anything: I think it builds up.

ODIE
So, is she telling people?

DOT
Nope. He is begging and says he will fix things, and she's nice to a fault.

ODIE
Hm, I knew someone like that once.

DOT
Cmon. I'm the baddest bitch now.

ODIE (smirking)
That is the goal.

DOT
I don't know what to do, I don't want her to believe him.

ODIE
So nobody outside of the little circle knows?

DOT
No, I desperately want the world to know.

ODIE
And he obviously doesn't. Does she?

DOT
I'm not sure at this point. (CONT'D) He still... (sighs) gets away with everything.

ODIE
Is she still scared? Are you?

DOT
I am the opposite of scared. But I'm not stupid, I know what he is capable of.

ODIE
So what can you do now?

DOT
I can't tell anybody if she won't let me, I can't make any of it stop, still. (CONT'D) It continues to hurt her.

ODIE
So, destroy his suits?

DOT
Yeah, (PAUSE) then kill him.

ODIE
Naturally.

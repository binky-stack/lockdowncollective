Ari & Joan: act one

by Josh Frydman and Olivia

ARI
Crew-leak

JOAN
*gesturing*
Crow-leek...

ARI
Like i said, crew le-e-ek

JOAN
Forget it. Neither of us have the tongue for this.

ARI
Hold on, I'm not wearing my glasses, one more time.

JOAN
Okay but you’re on your own.

ARI
*quietly, to himself*
Crow . leak. Crow leak.. Leak.
*finally gets the polish-sound*
crewleak.

*bus noise*

JOAN
I think we should move in together.

ARI
I-- I cant talk about this. Just can’t. Don’t push, ok?

JOAN
Is it because I’m not funny anymore?

ARI
No, it’s not that.

JOAN
Is it because I don’t like Adele?

ARI
No.

JOAN
Is it because I threw away that shirt? The one with the holes and the mud?

ARI
no.

JOAN
But kind of? I washed it twice.

ARI
Sort of, but no, not really.

JOAN
I’m going to go home and play piano for a little. Do you want to get dinner, after?

ARI
Actually, can you play at my grandmas? She wants to see us, I just remembered, sorry.

JOANS
Sure, I get that. Mine does too but whenever *shrug*. She wants us to sit in her new car.

ARI
Ok sure. We can do that… Sometime.

JOAN
You have to be the one to notice how blue it is.
‘Cuz she told me in her letter that it was electric blue and then we had that forty minute phone call and I filled up most of it with talk of our preferred shades of blue.
We agreed that we both like electric and that’s where it peaked.

ARI
Do you think I could ask her for one of her yoghurts this time?

JOAN
The digestive-aid? No, we pretend she doesn’t have to buy those.

ARI
Oh, then I can't. Sorry. I like having something to look forward to...

JOAN
Okay. I'm on my own then.

ARI
Are you upset?

JOAN
Shh. I’m ignoring you.

ARI
Oh no! Things were just nice what happened!

JOAN
No, they weren't. I did you the favor of changing the subject when you wouldn’t answer me-
*bus noise again* *they wait*
-and then you made things difficult.

ARI
No! I am a joy! You're difficult!

JOAN
*visibly hurt*
I didnt call you difficult why would you call me that???

ARI
What! what! whaaaat!!!!!!
You implied it! And you’re making accusations! How am i supposed to defend myself?

JOAN
You’re not on the witness stand, Ari! You don't need to defend yourself, you just need to agree with me!!

ARI
But I don’t agree, so where does that leave us?

JOAN
..it does not have to be this hard.

ARI
You are making this hell, I am in hell.

JOAN
I am in hell! You haven't moved in yet!

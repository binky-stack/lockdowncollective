4:00 PM

by Beverly Frydman

On the sofa with a cup of tea
no sugar just a drop of whiskey
I invite the fear to join me
and hope it knows when to leave

I wear furry Birkenstocks
with mismatched socks
not a fashion choice
just morning colour blindness

What am I waiting for?
This used to be the beginning
of the end of the day
now it’s just the hour after

another hour, no plan in sight
I sip my tea, for now it’s right
